Foundation
The gap that Street Legacy fills
The Problem
Life sims are too shallow

Most life sims offer tap-and-forget events with no real consequence system — choices don't compound across systems over time.

Legal and criminal systems are afterthoughts — a single popup replaces what should be an immersive arrest-to-courtroom experience.

Death is the game over screen. Legacy means nothing. The family you built, the wealth you accumulated — gone.

Players want authentic consequence — the feeling that their story is genuinely theirs.

The Solution
12 systems, all connected
✓

Cascading consequences — school trouble limits careers; criminal records block housing; neighborhood shapes every starting opportunity.

✓

Full legal UI pipeline — Arrest → Bail screen → 5-tier lawyer selection → Courtroom → Verdict. Every button pressable, every screen immersive.

✓

True generational play — death is not the end. Continue as a child, carry forward family wealth, reputation, and Legacy Score.

Born in the Hood or the Luxury District — every starting point unlocks a different story arc.
